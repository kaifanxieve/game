
# HOI4 Leader Traits Database

Extracted and compiled from Hearts of Iron IV, covering all leader traits with Chinese and English versions.

## Files

- traits_CN.txt — Chinese version with trait ID, Chinese name, and Chinese effect description
- traits_EN.txt — English version with trait ID and raw effect keys

## Data Sources

- Game data: Hearts of Iron IV, copyright Paradox Interactive
- Chinese localisation: Official Simplified Chinese localisation files
- Data compilation and translation mapping: Xiao Fan

## Coverage

- 1851 leader traits
- Sources: 00_traits, toa_traits, goe_traits

# HOI4 领袖特质数据库

摘录自《钢铁雄心4》，涵盖所有领袖特质，提供中英文双版本。

## 文件说明

- traits_CN.txt — 中文版，含特质ID、中文名称、中文效果描述
- traits_EN.txt — 英文版，含特质ID及原始效果key

## 数据来源

- 游戏数据：Hearts of Iron IV，版权归 Paradox Interactive 所有
- 中文本地化：游戏官方简体中文本地化文件
- 数据整理与翻译对照：小烦

## 覆盖范围

- 1851条领袖特质
- 来源文件：00_traits、toa_traits、goe_traits
